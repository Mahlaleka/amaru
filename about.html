AMARU PHOTOGRAPHY

Amaru Photography was registered with the Government of Malawi on 10th October 2012. Over the years, Amaru has gained vast experience and mastered the art of capturing great photographs. Amaru has achieved amazing results by working with various institutions, companies and non-governmental organizations including Save the Children, WaterAid, World Relief Malawi, Cathelic Relief Services, Palliduim, Old Mutual Malawi and Telekom Networks Malawi just to mention few. Amaru has been awarded several international and local prizes for its photography expertise and artistry exhibition including Humans of Malawi, Misuku Hill Art Challenge and World Bank Photography Competition among others.

As a Malawian photography and videography business with innovative concepts, we create high profile productions including video documentaries of full high definition for projects and/or events designed for websites, promotions, publicity, campaigns, fundraising and other purposes. Amaru offers services for local and international clients including individuals, families, companies and organizations. Amaru’s work has been recognized internationally. .

We also create, preserve and make great photographs that can be used as a powerful tool for communication on the website, annual reports, social media pages such as Facebook and twitter, publications and success stories, among others. These photographs are of high quality and also capture moments to tell a story..
